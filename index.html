Rebirth Areté

Day one, every day.

Rebirth is the daily chance to burn away things that are old: habits, routines and stories that once served you but now keep you stuck.

Areté is about demanding the best for ourselves without pretending we have to be perfect. Every day is like day one - a fresh opportunity to return to the standard you know you are capable of.

There’s something inside you that knows you could be doing better. This work starts by listening to that part instead of arguing with it.

It’s got very little to do with information. Most people already know the basics: eat better, move more, train consistently. The harder part is doing it when motivation fades, life gets heavy and the old stories come back.

Coaching is about unravelling the reasons why we got here in the first place, then building the habits and accountability to move differently from here.
