Why it sticks

Why standard training fails

Most people do not need more fitness information. They already know the basics: train consistently, eat better, move more and sleep properly.

The real problem is the habits, routines and stories that keep pulling them back to the same place. The stories we tell ourselves about why this won’t work can become stronger than the plan itself.

There’s something inside you that knows you could be doing better. This work starts there, with honest conversation instead of another restart.

Rebirth is the daily chance to start again: burning away things that are old and outgrowing habits that no longer serve you. Every day is like day one - a fresh opportunity.

Areté is holding yourself to a higher standard without pretending you have to be perfect. It is demanding the best for ourselves, calmly and consistently.
